About Me

I am Ahmed. I am from a small village in West Sudan. I lived almost my entire life there. Only after I was accepted in university did I travel to Khartoum. My life has never been easy. Since an early age I have begun working and supporting my family, even in university holidays I had to returen to my home village and work.

Despite all that, I am still ambitious and working towards big dreams. My recent graduation from Sudan University of Sceince and Technology with first class honour in Electronics Engineering is just the beginning.
